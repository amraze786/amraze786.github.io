Where it all began

After working for several years as a Manager in retail, it was time for a exciting change in career! Thats when I aimed to be a successful developer!

My Inspiration

It hit me one evening when I realized I wasn't feeling fulfilled with being in retail for a really long time. I learned of multiple bootcamps offering courses on programming and thats when I looked into what coding actually is. When I learned about the structure behind how some of the most major and popular websites are built, curiousty got the best of me and thats when I dived into know more and to apply these techniques for sites and apps I would love to build.

My Education

I joined NuCamp which is a bootcamp that focuses on learning how to code. Through Nucamp I learned various programming languages and also applied the knowledge into weekly workshop projects. Apart from NuCamp, I also used YouTube and FreeCodeCamp as resources to better understand the languages I was learning and to also learn more other langauges that can assist me better in the future.

Traveling

"Investment in travel is in investment for yourself"

I truly feel that one benefits a lot from traveing as it gives the individual an experience to learn, to express, and most of all to feel. I love taking roadtrips as it gives more time to fully feel the surroundings and a chance to just let go.

Nature

Did you know nature has the most powerful ability to heal the deepest of deepest wounds of the soul? It is proven that spending a good amount of time everyday with nature highly reduces negative emotions such as anger, grief, depression, anxiety. Not only does nature contribute to an overall health of the mind and soul, it also contributes to physical health with numerous benefits such as reduced blood pressure, muscle tension, and stress hormones. Whenever I get the time, I visit the waterfalls to enjoy the peace and serenity which in return leaves a profound effect on my well-being.
